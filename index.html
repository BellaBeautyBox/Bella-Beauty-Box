Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay a deposit?
Yes a deposit of 25% is required to secure all bookings.
What is your cancellation policy?
The deposit although is non-refundable can be used for another party in the future.
What ages are the parties suitable for?
Our parties are perfect for children aged 5 and up, teens, and adults who love some glam fun!
How many guests can I invite?
We can cater for small groups of 4–12 guests. We might be able to cater for larger events, contact us to discuss further.
Do you travel outside Limerick?
Yes! We cover Limerick, Clare, Tipperary, and surrounding areas. A small travel fee may apply for longer distances.
Are the products safe for children?
All products used are gentle and child-friendly. Please list any allergies in the consent form.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 3–4 weeks in advance to secure your date.
Is there anything I need to provide?
Yes, there will need to be an open space to park the venue on a flat surface and access to an electrical socket.
Will you take videos and photos?
Yes, with consent. Videos and photos will be taken and sent on to the person who booked the party.
What happens during bad weather?
Bella Beauty Box is fully enclosed and can operate in most weather conditions. However, for severe weather, we will need to reschedule your party to a safer date.
