Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay a deposit?
Yes — 25% is required to secure bookings.
What is your cancellation policy?
Deposit is non-refundable but can be used for a future party.
What ages are the parties suitable for?
Ages 5+, teens, and adults.
How many guests can I invite?
We cater for 4–12 guests (contact us for larger groups).
Do you travel outside Limerick?
Yes — Limerick, Clare, Tipperary & surrounds (travel fee may apply).
Are the products safe for children?
Yes — child-friendly products; list allergies on the consent form.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend 3–4 weeks in advance.
Is there anything I need to provide?
An open, flat space to park and access to an electrical socket.
Will you take videos and photos?
Yes — with consent; they’ll be sent to the person who booked.
What happens during bad weather?
We’re enclosed and can operate in most weather; severe weather may require reschedule.
